Business Requirements Document (BRD)

Automation for Startup Apparel Store

Prepared by Ankur Madan

Executive Summary

This BRD outlines the plan for automating inventory management, POS, CRM, and reporting systems in a startup apparel store to improve efficiency and reduce operational friction.

Problem Statement

Manual operations (e.g., stock updates, sales logging, and customer outreach) lead to inefficiencies, stock errors, poor customer engagement, and lack of insights into business performance.

Business Objectives

  • Automate inventory with real-time stock updates
  • Digitize POS for accurate and timely sales reporting
  • Automate customer follow-ups using CRM tools
  • Provide dashboard-based KPI tracking

Scope Definition

In-Scope:

  • Barcode-based inventory tracking
  • POS integration with automated reports
  • Customer data capture and automated emails
  • Dashboard for operational insights

Out-of-Scope:

  • Mobile e-commerce development
  • Multi-branch support or international logistics
